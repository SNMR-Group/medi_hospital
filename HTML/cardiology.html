Medi Hospital's cardiology department provides a comprehensive range of services aimed at diagnosing, treating, and managing various heart-related conditions. Their emergency care unit stands ready to respond to acute cardiac emergencies, ensuring prompt and lifesaving interventions for patients experiencing heart attacks or other critical situations. Diagnostic testing forms a cornerstone of their cardiology services, with state-of-the-art equipment enabling the performance of electrocardiograms (ECGs), echocardiograms, stress tests, cardiac catheterization, and advanced imaging scans like cardiac MRI or CT scans.


Once a diagnosis is established, Medi Hospital offers a spectrum of treatment options, including medication management, lifestyle counseling, and interventional procedures such as angioplasty, stent placement, and pacemaker/defibrillator implantation. For more complex cases, their cardiac surgery department stands prepared to perform intricate procedures like coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG), valve repair or replacement surgery, and heart transplantations.


Complementing these acute interventions, Medi Hospital also provides comprehensive cardiac rehabilitation services to aid patients in their recovery and promote long-term heart health. With a focus on preventive care, their cardiology department emphasizes risk factor assessment, lifestyle counseling, and proactive programs aimed at averting heart disease and fostering heart-healthy habits among patients.
